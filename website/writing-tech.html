Ready Player One Argumentative Essay

Ready Player One by Earnest Cline should be a standard book in high school English classes today. The novel is more relatable to today’s teens, is a good example of escapism, and takes place in a future that is very closely related to today’s issues. I am firm in my belief that this book should be read in high school English classes because it’s relatable and relevant to teens while still providing teaching material.
